# Prayer

## Liturgy and Devotion

Prayer is the raising of one's mind and heart to God or the requesting of good things from God.

## Forms of Prayer

- **Liturgical Prayer** — The official prayer of the Church (Liturgy of the Hours, Mass)
- **Personal Prayer** — Individual conversation with God
- **Contemplative Prayer** — Silent union with God
- **Devotional Prayer** — Rosary, novenas, litanies
